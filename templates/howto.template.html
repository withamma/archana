How To

How does it work?

  1. See Question mark
  2. Say verse _From Memory_
  3. Click Check to see actual answer
  4. If answer was correct, click green checkmark, else click red x (Your wrong answers are recorded)
  5. If you accidently clicked wrong button, click undo button, or swipe left on the screen if you're on a phone
  6. Repeat until all names are finished
  7. Follow link to see results
  8. Export Missed results to quizlet (don't need quizlet account - if you have one you can copy the cards to your deck)

May Amma's grace be with you =)
